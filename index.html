Is cert X worth taking?

. Do you have gaps in your knowledge you want to fill? . Does a potential employer value the certification? . Do you have access to a training budget and can get it paid? . Are you able to demonstrate compentency in the area without it?

If any of these answers are YES, then you may want to study up and take the exam.
